Prelude

My love for Thee is an Unending River.

Written by Your Beloved on July 25, 2016

Life now is all about technology. It sucks that people get stuck staring at the screens of their own cellular devices and those electronic beeps and boops. Hooked to the trend, still technology is everything. I get to talk to you through this channel. It even helps me look for remedies for certain ailments when I do not get to experience your tender embraces and sweet kisses. Whee! I even started cooking with the aid of wikipedia.com so I can provide you your favorites when the time comes we get to be together.

From this day forward, due to our separating ways, I will be putting my concerns and big days here (for you). Food brings us to different places and introduces our tongues to new tastes.They say one will surely visit a place, to relax, admire and discover new surroundings within, to find and dig on the good stuff.

Despite being apart, new experiences and activities I shall bestow upon thee.

Yet, you won't miss the important days of my life. I'll keep you posted on my adventures within the vicinity of my university, through the city and around the world. Though I may not always find the opportunity to be with you, know that this serves as my underlying care and love and for you.
